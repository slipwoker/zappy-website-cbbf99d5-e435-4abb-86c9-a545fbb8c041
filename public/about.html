    Our Connection to Coral Reefs

    The name Coral reflects our deep appreciation for the natural world's artistry and our commitment to preserving it. Coral reefs are among Earth's most biodiverse and valuable ecosystems, yet they face unprecedented threats from climate change, pollution, and human activity.
